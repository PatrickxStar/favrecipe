Step by Step Directions to Make Chicken Alfredo!

Directions

*Follow each step in numerical order*


  1. Cook the pasta in boiling water.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, prepare the chicken by seasoning it and cooking it in a pan.
  3. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the pan and set it aside.
  4. In the same pan, make the Alfredo sauce by melting butter, adding minced garlic, pouring in heavy cream, and stirring in grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Add the cooked pasta and chicken to the sauce, and mix everything together until heated through.
  6. Serve hot, optionally garnished with chopped parsley and additional grated Parmesan cheese.
Ingredients Nutritional Value
1/2 lbs Chicken Breast Protein 100g
1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese Sodium 100mg
1/4 cup of olive oil Calories 100mg
1/2 stick of butter Cholesterol 25mg
1 pack of frozen garlic bread Total Carbohydrate 25g
