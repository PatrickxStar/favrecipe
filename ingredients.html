Ingredients to Make Chicken Alfredo!

  • 1/2 lbs Chicken Breast
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 pack of frozen garlic bread
  • Clove of garlic
  • Parsley
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • Half quart of heavy cream
  • 2 cans of Alfredo sauce
